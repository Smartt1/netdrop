About Us

WHAT WE DO


Netdrop skills is an online skills acquisition centre and a digital intermediary that links publishers and creators of high quality ebooks to customers that are interested in buying them.

 

Netdrop skills was created and introduced in 2023 when the need to acquire specific skills became a noticeable need in the neighboring societies around.

 

Netdrop skills also allows for effective publication of various Ebooks by users on any topic of their choices and also encourages the marketing and promotions of those set Ebooks by other users for commissions at the end of the period.

 

The skills can be acquired by all users from their dashboards once they become a user and meets the criteria for those skills set.

 

Divers skills set are made available and experts are readily accessible to learn from and gain expertise on those skills set. 

 

On NETDROP, Various users can affiliate the skills centre to other non users, publish an ebook for resale or promote any Ebook of their choice for stated commissions.
